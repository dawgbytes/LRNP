Our Process

We follow a systematic approach to ensure your native plant project is successful from start to finish:

1. Initial Consultation

We start with a thorough consultation to understand your goals, site conditions, and project requirements.

2. Site Assessment

Our team evaluates soil conditions, drainage, sun exposure, and existing vegetation to create the perfect plant selection.

3. Design & Planning

We develop a detailed plan including plant selection, placement, timing, and maintenance requirements.

4. Plant Sourcing

We source high-quality native plants from our nursery and trusted partners, ensuring genetic authenticity and plant health.

5. Installation & Delivery

Professional installation and delivery services ensure your plants are properly placed and cared for from day one.

6. Follow-up Support

We provide ongoing support, care guidance, and maintenance recommendations to ensure long-term success.
