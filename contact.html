Meet Jep Whitlock

Owner & Founder

With over two decades of experience in native plant cultivation and wetland restoration, Jep Whitlock founded Lumber River Native Plants to provide the highest quality native plants to the Midwest and Southeast regions.

Jep's expertise includes aquatic plant selection, wetland ecosystem restoration, and native landscaping design. He works directly with customers to ensure they receive the perfect plants for their specific needs and growing conditions.

Contact Jep directly for large projects, custom plant selections, or specialized consultation services.
