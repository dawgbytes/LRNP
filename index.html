About Lumber River Native Plants

Founded by Jep Whitlock, Lumber River Native Plants has been serving the Midwest and Southeast as the premier native plant nursery for over two decades. We specialize in aquatic plants, wetland species, and native landscaping solutions that support local ecosystems.

Our nursery in Gibson, NC, is strategically located to serve the unique needs of the Lumber River region, providing plants specifically adapted to local conditions and water systems.
