Popular Seed Kits

Pollinator Meadow Mix

Blend of native perennials for season-long bloom. Tested germination ≥85%, purity ≥98%.

For pond edges and rain gardens. Tested germination ≥80%, purity ≥97%.

Native Tree Seed Pack

Regionally appropriate tree species with stratification guidance. Germination and purity tested.

How Dropship Seeds Work

  1. You request a quote and provide your shipping address
  2. We confirm availability and final pricing with our seed partner
  3. You pay our invoice; we place the supplier order to ship directly to you
  4. You receive tracking and a seed lot data sheet (germination, purity, test date)

Quality: We only partner with suppliers who provide documented germination %, purity %, test dates, and noxious-weed-free attestations.
